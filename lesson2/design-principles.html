Visual Hierarchy

Duolingo

https://www.duolingo.com/

When you first enter the Duolingo home page it uses visual hierarchy by the use of colors and the position of elements, such as the site name on top, or by using an outstanding color for the "Get started" button. They also make the short description of Duolingo a little bigger to highlight their attributes.

PARC: Repetition

Khan Academy

https://www.khanacademy.org/

In Khan Academy, when you are navigating through the courses you can notice how they use repetition so you can easily distinguish course topics and material. They repeat the same grouping format for every course, and although using different icons, they repeat the colors to convey the idea that topics are related to the same course.

PARC: Contrast

BYU Pathway Worldwide

https://www.byupathway.org/

In BYU Pathway Worldwide the way they contrast a little message with the background image is by using white letters in front of a darker image. You can also notice how that image is extra darkened to add contrast with the letters. Also, using yellow in the graph contrast not only the color but the idea of how Pathway is the best option.
