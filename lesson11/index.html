Bicycle Sundays Event

Join us and take care of the planet!

Many of the things we enjoy nowadays thanks to technology have become harmful to the planet. Day after day many people around the globe can enjoy the wonders of technology as they become more and more affordable. The demand goes up but at the same time, those harmful effects grow and the climate change goes crazy. What could we do about it?

Fortunately, there's much we can do about it. Though little, these changes in our lifestyle will make a great difference in improving the health of our planet.

That's why we wanted to invite you to our new event where we will join to make the use of bicycles a healthy habit for both our planet and ourselves. The plan is simple, you just need to do your best to use your bicycle all day on Sundays starting next month. Also, we invite you to share your photos and experiences using the email information provided below with the subject "Bicycle Event," so we can share your experiences in future articles.
