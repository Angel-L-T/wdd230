Our Purpose and Goals

The purpose of the “Nezahualcóyotl Chamber of Commerce” is to connect people and local businesses to foster healthy commerce in Nezahualcóyotl city. We achieve this, first, by giving our members a space where they can showcase their products, solutions, or services through our website. Second, we provide helpful, easy to access information on our website for people so they can know the different business solutions in the city.

Our Members

Metro Candy Shop

Maybe you have a small business and you want to get good prices to resupply your store. Or maybe you will have a party for your kids and you want a lot of candy for all of their friends. Whatever your candy needs are, Metro Candy shop has everything that you are looking for.

Charly's Pizza

There are many places to eat pizza in the city, but there are few in which almost every group of people will feel comfortable. The restaurant has places for big families, small groups of friends, or people that just want to have a good slice of pizza.

El Diamante Negro Saloon

When you need to find a place for a big party it is sometimes difficult to find a good spot with clean and modern buildings. That's what we have decided to save you some time and introduce you to the Diamante Negro saloon. In this saloon the party might last a few hours, but the good memories will last forever.
