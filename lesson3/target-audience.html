Target Audience

Clear Weather Target Audience

The target audience of Clear Weather are:

  • Who: People planning their daily activities based on local weather conditions.
  • Age: 15 to 100
  • Motivation: Prepare themselves as they travel and enjoy their planned activities.

Personas

Alan Wild

Age: 25

Occupation: College student, pursuing a BS in agriculture.

Frequent activities: He enjoys hiking with his college friends in his free time. He also goes to the supermarket for supplies every weekend. Most of his schoolwork is outside in the countryside.

Andrew Smith

Age: 45

Occupation: Working full time as a maintenance manager for a couple of local supermarkets.

Frequent activities: Every day he travels to the different supermarkets for scheduled maintenance tasks. He also enjoys spending his weekends with his family on different activities such as hiking, visiting the park, going to the movies, or playing in the yard.

Scenarios

  • Should I pack my umbrella today?
  • What time should I go to the supermarket today?
  • It is raining, how can we spend our time today as a family?
  • I have free time on Sunday, where should we go hiking today?
  • Is it a good idea to prepare a barbecue on Saturday?
