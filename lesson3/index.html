Site Purpose

The purpose of clear weather is to provide local, accurate, and current weather information to keep people aware of weather conditions when planning their daily activities. To fulfill our purpose, we will simplify weather information by making it easily interpretable. We will also provide different articles with ideas for activities that our visitors could perform given the weather conditions.
